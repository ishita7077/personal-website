# Yale School of Management — answer framework

## Handoff structure (from mba_final_handoff_static/deep-research-report (2).md)

**Validated format (paraphrased from handoff):** Blind ~30 minute interview + separate video questions (two; ~60 seconds each in handoff).

**Distinctive elements:** Video assesses communication; live interview resume-only.

**Interviewer profile (handoff):** Second-year student, recent alum, or admissions committee member

**Community / texture note:** Separate components for video vs blind live (handoff).

## How to answer (shared)

1. Answer the question first; then illustrate.
2. Use one strong example with your role, tradeoffs, and outcome.
3. Reflect on learning or how it informs MBA goals.
4. For simulations (TBD, Kira, video timers), respect the stated time box in your practice.
