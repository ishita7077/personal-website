# Stanford GSB — answer framework

## Handoff structure (from mba_final_handoff_static/deep-research-report (2).md)

**Validated format (paraphrased from handoff):** 1:1 structured behavioural interview focused on past actions; trained alumni or admissions; ~45–60 minutes; deep probing.

**Distinctive elements:** All admits interview; emphasis on recent meaningful professional/community experiences; depth over breadth (per handoff report).

**Interviewer profile (handoff):** Alumni subset or MBA Admissions team

**Community / texture note:** Community reports emphasize deep follow-ups and substance over polish (step5 community notes).

## How to answer (shared)

1. Answer the question first; then illustrate.
2. Use one strong example with your role, tradeoffs, and outcome.
3. Reflect on learning or how it informs MBA goals.
4. For simulations (TBD, Kira, video timers), respect the stated time box in your practice.
