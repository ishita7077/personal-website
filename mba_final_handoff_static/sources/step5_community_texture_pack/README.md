# Step 5 Community Texture Pack

This pack contains community/applicant-texture sources for the 10 target MBA schools.
Per school it includes:
- community-sources.csv
- community-notes.md

These sources are intentionally lower-confidence than official or school-affiliated sources. They are meant to capture recurring interview patterns, candidate-reported tone, surprises, likely follow-up behavior, and implementation-useful texture.
They must not override official facts.
