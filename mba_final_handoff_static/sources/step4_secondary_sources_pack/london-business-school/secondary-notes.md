# London Business School — secondary notes

Secondary-source take:
- LBS has one of the better school-affiliated secondary ecosystems because the admissions blog is active and clearly tied to the admissions journey.
- This is useful for both tone and product realism: LBS wants candidates to research deeply, use team support, understand career goals, and learn from student stories.
- Compared with some U.S. peers, LBS’s school-specific distinctiveness can be built strongly from these school-owned blogs.

What this adds beyond Step 3:
- Substantial material for school-specific resource packs.
- Better support for a mature, globally oriented, goals-heavy LBS experience in product.
